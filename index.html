Mechanics

  • Time Cloning: Press SPACEBAR (or Echo button) to deploy an Echo of your past 3 seconds. It loops infinitely.
  • Adaptive Nodes: Cores flee from YOU, but are attracted to your ECHOS. Herd them using your past selves.
  • Phase Shifts: Every 15s, the rules change:
    - GATHER Phase: Herd cores into the central Nexus.
    - HUNT Phase: Nexus repels cores. You must touch cores directly.
  • Learning AI: Red Hunters predict your future position based on velocity. Confuse their algorithm by deploying an Echo before sharply changing direction.
